Sahyadri Electricals

Powering Progress with Innovative Solutions

Founded in 2007 by a group of five visionary entrepreneurs, SE has established itself as a premier manufacturer and service provider in the electrical industry. Our journey over the past decade is a testament to our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

At SE, we pride ourselves on our sophisticated range of products and services, catering to a diverse clientele who trust us for our high-quality solutions. We operate across three pivotal areas of the power sector:

Manufacturing

Crafting state-of-the-art electrical components that power industries.

Power Management Solutions and Projects

Specializing in turn-key projects that streamline operations from conception to execution.

Operations and Maintenance

Ensuring the longevity and efficiency of electrical systems through meticulous care.

Services

Projects Division

Our Projects Division is at the forefront of power management, handling projects from design to execution. Design & Engineering:

We offer full design packages, encompassing engineering and drafting for new builds, refits, and electrical systems. Our design capabilities include :

  • Complete design packages
  • New build electrical system design (AC and DC)
  • Refit electrical design
  • Switchboard, starter, and control panel design
  • Alarm and monitoring system design
  • Generator control system design
  • Electrical power generation, protection, and distribution design

Installation Services:

  • Precision in cable supply, installation, and termination.
  • Full-scale electrical system installations.
  • Control system and air conditioning/refrigeration electrical systems

Maintenance & Repair:

  • Rapid emergency response and repair services.
  • Maintenance and repair of electrical power generation, protection, and distribution systems.
  • Mechanical services and rotating machinery repair.

Liaison Services:

  • Specialized liaison services with MSEDCL, MSETCL, and Mahagenco.
  • Engagement with all government agencies.
